Cookies

When you visit the Paran property website we place ‘cookies’ also known as ‘data files’ on the computer or device you are using. This is an activity undertaken by many other websites.

When you load a browser, cookies are created from the website which send information in the form of a ‘text file’ to your browser. These cookies are stored on your device for varying periods of time. When you come back to the website, the browser will retrieve cookies and send them to the server. This is helpful for not having to re-enter details each time you visit the site.

Cookies enable us to track what users do by tracing activity while you are on the website. They provide insights as to how many people have visited the site and which pages are the most visited. Ultimately, this allows us to provide you with a better experience when you next visit our website.

We use different types of cookies. These are: strictly necessary cookies, functionality cookies, performance cookies and targeting cookies. If you want to know more about cookies, please visit www.allabutcookies.org If you wish to block cookies, you can use the setting on your browser that allows you to block cookies. If you block cookies using your browser, please bear in mind you will may not be able to access all areas of our website.
